About Me

I’m a product designer with a focus on UI,UX and visual design. With 8+ years of experience, I take products from the ideation stage to the launch stage.

I have worked on a variety of projects which include, AI, Edutech, Environment, College Safety and Intersex Community. I have also designed products based on OCR, automated scheduling and data management.

Some organizations that I have worked for include Derek O’Brien & Associates, Xane AI, Jhatkaa.org, Fairtrade, Harper Collins Publishers, etc.

I founded a textile based product brand and leveraged technology extensively to not just market and sell, but also manage all business related operations.

I was the winner of NTC's Young Designer Hunt 2013.

English and Hindi are my mother tongue. I am also learning German (currently at A2 level).
